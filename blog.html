  • The modern train - ER1

    Stadler KISS, also known as Dosto in Sweden, is a two-story motor coach train manufactured by the Swiss train company Stadler. The motor coach was originally called Dosto, from the German word "Doppelstock" meaning double decker. Since 2010, Stadler has referred to it as KISS, an acronym for "komfortabler innovativer spurtstarker S-Bahn-Zug" which means "comfortably, innovatively, fast suburban train". The train comes in various lengths, up to six cars, and has been sold to several countries in Europe and North America.
